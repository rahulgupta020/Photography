About

yogesh Bhatanagar photographer

We are a 4 year old professional photography services company, specializing in Hindu Wedding, Destination Wedding, Engagement, Reception, Marwadi Wedding, Rajasthani Wedding, Punjabi Wedding, Gujarati Wedding, Pre-wedding Shoots, Maharashtrian Wedding, Indian Wedding, South Indian Wedding, Wedding, New Born, Birthday, Kids Portraits, Babies & Kids, Conferences and Corporate Events photography. We offer products and services such as HD Videos, Cinematography, Photobook, HD Highlights and CD / DVD. We accept customized photography requirements. Our studio is in Mumbai. We cover events in Maharashtra, Rajasthan, Gujarat and Goa. We are comfortable communicating in English, Marathi and Hindi.

yogesh Bhatanagar is a Delhi and Bangalore based photographer, working mainly in the field of lifestyle, advertising and travel photography. HIs clients include all major publications in the country and many national and international brands. Also, shooting street life and portraits has been his passion for over two decades now.
